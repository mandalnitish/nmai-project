Welcome to NMAI Current Affairs

NMAI Current Affairs (nmai.in) is India's premier online platform dedicated to helping aspirants of competitive examinations stay updated with daily current affairs, practice MCQs, and master the art of exam preparation. We are committed to providing high-quality, exam-oriented content that empowers millions of students across India to achieve their dreams of serving the nation.

Our Story

Founded in [Year], NMAI Current Affairs was born out of a simple yet powerful vision: to make quality current affairs content accessible to every competitive exam aspirant in India, regardless of their location or economic background.

Our journey began when our founders, themselves successful civil service professionals, recognized a critical gap in the market. While countless students were dedicating their lives to preparing for UPSC, SSC, Banking, State PSC, and other competitive examinations, they lacked a reliable, comprehensive, and affordable source of daily current affairs tailored specifically to their exam requirements.

What started as a small blog sharing daily news summaries has evolved into a comprehensive educational platform serving hundreds of thousands of aspirants monthly. Today, NMAI Current Affairs stands as a trusted companion in the preparation journey of students from Kashmir to Kanyakumari, from Gujarat to the Northeast.
